Business & Financial Profile Highlights** #### **Business & Operations** * **Incorporated:** 9 May 2007. * **Business Model:** Manufactures and markets products for soil health, crop nutrition, and crop protection (pesticides via wholly-owned subsidiary Nova Agri Sciences Pvt Ltd). * **Geographic Reach:** Well-established presence across **18 states** in India. * **Top Markets (FY25 Consolidated Sales):** Telangana (\~55%), Andhra Pradesh (23%), and Karnataka (6%). * **Distribution Network:** Extensive dealer network comprising approximately **10,900 dealers**, of which approximately **6,200 are active**. * **Product Portfolio:** Offers a diversified branded product portfolio, including soil health management, crop nutrition, and crop protection products. As of November 2023, had **720 product registrations**. #### **Key Financial Risks** * **Working Capital Intensity:** Operations are intensive due to an **elongated operating cycle**, which stood at **217 days in FY25** (vs. 203 days in FY24). * **Debtor Days:** High at **224 days in FY25** (vs. 178 days in FY24). The company offers a credit period of $\\sim$4–5 months to customers. * **Working Capital Utilisation:** Average utilisation of fund-based working capital limits remains high at $\\sim$**93–96%** (for 12 months ended May 2025\). * **Profitability Moderation:** The PBILDT margin declined from $17.56\\%$ in FY24 to $15.22\\%$ in FY25, primarily due to an increase in **raw material (RM) costs** (RM expenses as a percentage of total income rose to $69\\%$ in FY25 from $54\\%$ in FY24). * **Liquidity:** Assessed as **adequate** with Gross Cash Accruals (GCA) of ₹**30.43 crore** against term loan repayment obligations of ₹**5.51 crore** and liquid investments of ₹**29.36 crore** as on March 31, 2025. ### --- **3\. Conclusion for Lender** The company exhibits a **healthy financial risk profile** marked by low leverage and strong debt coverage metrics post-IPO, supported by an established track record and diversified product range. The **primary constraint** for a lender is the **intensive and elongated working capital cycle** (217 days) and **high utilisation of fund-based limits** ($\\sim$93–96%), which necessitates **tight monitoring of collections and working capital movement**.
