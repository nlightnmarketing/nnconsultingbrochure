1. What is AIEO?

AI Engine Optimization (AIEO) is the next evolution of SEO. As more users turn to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini for information, businesses need to optimize their content to appear in these AI-generated responses.

Key Differences: SEO vs AIEO

  • SEO: Optimizes for search engines (Google, Bing) - focuses on keywords, backlinks, technical SEO
  • AIEO: Optimizes for AI platforms - focuses on structured data, entity relationships, authoritative content, and comprehensive information

Why AIEO Matters for Real Estate

  • Users increasingly ask AI: "Best apartments in Electronic City" or "Puravankara project reviews"
  • AI assistants provide direct answers, bypassing traditional search results
  • Early AIEO adopters gain significant competitive advantage
  • AI platforms prefer authoritative, well-structured, comprehensive content
