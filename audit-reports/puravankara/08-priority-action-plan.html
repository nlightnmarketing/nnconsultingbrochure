Priority Action Plan (Next 90 Days)

Puravankara Limited | Generated by NLightN Consulting | Date: January 2025

Executive Summary

This 90-day action plan prioritizes the most critical digital marketing improvements for Puravankara based on comprehensive audit findings. The plan is structured in three phases, focusing on quick wins, growth experiments, and optimization for sustainable results.

Overall Digital Maturity Rating: 6.5/10 (Moderate)

Path Forward: By implementing this action plan, Puravankara can transform from a locally-known developer with strong organic presence into a digitally-dominant real estate brand with systematic lead generation, enhanced reputation, and scalable growth.

Phase 1: Foundation & Quick Wins (Days 0-30)

Focus: Activate dormant assets, implement critical SEO fixes, and establish foundational systems for growth.

1. Activate the Blog & Content Hub CRITICAL

Current State: Blog section exists but shows limited content

Action Items:

  • Publish 5 high-quality articles focused on key revenue drivers:
    • "Complete Guide to Buying Your First Home in Bangalore 2026"
    • "Sustainable Living: How Puravankara's Green Buildings Save You Money"
    • "Electronic City vs Whitefield: Which is Better for Your Lifestyle?"
    • "Investment Property Guide: Which Puravankara City Offers Best ROI?"
    • "NRI Home Buying Guide: Everything You Need to Know"
  • Optimize all blog posts with meta descriptions, title tags, and internal links
  • Add schema markup (Article schema) to all blog posts
  • Create content calendar for consistent publishing (2-3 posts per week)

Expected Impact: 20-30% increase in organic traffic, improved SEO rankings

2. SEO Hygiene & Technical Fixes CRITICAL

Action Items:

  • Add meta descriptions to all core service and project pages
  • Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across website, Google Business Profiles, and directories
  • Implement Review/Rating schema markup on homepage and project pages
  • Add descriptive alt tags to all images
  • Fix any broken links and optimize page load speed
  • Submit XML sitemap to Google Search Console

Expected Impact: Improved search visibility, better click-through rates

3. Set Up Google Business Profiles HIGH PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Create/optimize Google Business Profiles for all 9 office locations
  • Add high-quality photos, business hours, services, and attributes
  • Enable Q&A feature and respond to questions
  • Post regular updates (project launches, events, news)
  • Set up review request system targeting 10-15 reviews per location per month

Expected Impact: Improved local SEO, increased trust signals, more local leads

4. Implement Review Request System HIGH PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Set up automated SMS/WhatsApp review requests post-visit, post-booking, post-possession
  • Create review request templates with direct links to Google, Justdial, 99acres
  • Train sales team on review request best practices
  • Implement review response process (respond within 24-48 hours)

Expected Impact: 50-100+ reviews per month, improved reputation scores

5. Set Up Conversion Tracking CRITICAL

Action Items:

  • Implement Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with proper event tracking
  • Set up Google Tag Manager
  • Create conversion goals for: form submissions, WhatsApp clicks, app downloads, phone calls
  • Implement UTM parameters for campaign tracking
  • Set up call tracking for phone number conversions

Expected Impact: Data-driven decision making, ROI measurement

6. Website Enhancements HIGH PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Add email newsletter signup with lead magnet (e.g., "Free Home Buying Guide")
  • Implement chatbot for 24/7 lead qualification
  • Add FAQ sections to top 3 department/project pages
  • Create dedicated landing pages for paid advertising campaigns
  • Add social sharing buttons to blog posts

Expected Impact: Increased lead capture, better user experience

Phase 2: Growth Experiments (Days 30-60)

Focus: Launch paid advertising tests, enhance content distribution, and improve engagement across channels.

7. Launch Paid Advertising Test Campaigns HIGH PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Google Search Ads: Launch test campaign (₹2-3 lakh budget) targeting high-intent keywords:
    • "apartments in Electronic City"
    • "Puravankara projects Bangalore"
    • "luxury apartments in [city]"
  • Facebook/Instagram Ads: Launch lead generation campaigns (₹1.5-2.5 lakh budget) targeting:
    • Lookalike audiences based on existing customers
    • Retargeting website visitors
    • Interest-based targeting (real estate, home buying)
  • Create dedicated landing pages for each campaign
  • Set up conversion tracking and A/B testing

Expected Impact: 50-100 additional leads per month, scalable growth channel

8. Content Repurposing Engine MEDIUM PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Transform each blog article into:
    • 1 Instagram/Facebook carousel or reel
    • 1 LinkedIn article summary
    • 1 short YouTube/WhatsApp video explainer
    • 1 Twitter thread
  • Create content templates for consistent repurposing
  • Schedule content distribution across all platforms

Expected Impact: 3-5x content reach, better engagement, time efficiency

9. Social Media Engagement Boost MEDIUM PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Increase interactive content (polls, Q&As, behind-the-scenes) on Instagram/Facebook
  • Aim for 5% interaction rate (currently estimated lower)
  • Respond to all comments within 24 hours
  • Create LinkedIn Live sessions monthly for Q&A
  • Share user-generated content and customer stories

Expected Impact: 30-50% increase in engagement, stronger community

10. Email Marketing Program Launch HIGH PRIORITY

  • Launch monthly newsletter

11. WhatsApp Business Optimization HIGH PRIORITY

Action Items:

  • Set up WhatsApp Business API
