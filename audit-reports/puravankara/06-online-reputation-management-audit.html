8. Negative Review Management

Current State

Negative Review Response Process Not Identified

Recommended Negative Review Management Strategy

Response Framework:

  1. Respond Quickly: Within 24-48 hours
  2. Be Professional: Acknowledge the concern, apologize if warranted
  3. Take It Offline: Offer to discuss privately via phone/email
  4. Show Resolution: Demonstrate that issues are being addressed
  5. Follow Up: Ensure the issue is resolved and update the review if possible

Common Negative Review Scenarios (Real Estate Industry)

Pricing Issues Clarify pricing structure, explain value proposition Transparent pricing, clear communication

9. Review Generation Strategy

Automated Review Request System

Recommended Touchpoints for Review Requests:

  • Post-Visit (Site Visit): SMS/WhatsApp 24 hours after site visit
  • Post-Booking: Email/SMS after booking confirmation
  • Post-Possession: Email/SMS 30 days after possession
  • Post-Event: Email after attending project launch events
  • App Users: In-app prompts for app store reviews

Review Request Best Practices

  • Make it easy - provide direct links to review platforms
  • Personalize requests - use customer name and project name
  • Time it right - request when customer is most satisfied
  • Incentivize appropriately - offer value (not payment for reviews)
  • Follow up - gentle reminder if no review after 7 days
  • Thank reviewers - acknowledge and appreciate feedback
