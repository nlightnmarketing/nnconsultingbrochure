Executive Summary

This audit evaluates Puravankara's lead generation strategies and paid advertising presence. The analysis reveals a strong organic lead generation foundation through website, app, and WhatsApp, but limited visible paid advertising efforts that could significantly scale lead acquisition.

Overall Lead Generation Score: 62/100

Key Finding: Puravankara relies primarily on organic channels (website, directory listings, word-of-mouth). While this is sustainable, paid advertising presents significant opportunities to accelerate growth, target specific audiences, and compete more effectively in a crowded real estate market.

3. Paid Advertising Opportunities

Google Ads Strategy

Facebook & Instagram Ads

LinkedIn Ads
