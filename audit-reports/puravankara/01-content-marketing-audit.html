7. Recommendations

Immediate Actions (0-30 days)

  • Optimize existing blog posts with better meta descriptions and title tags
  • Add schema markup for real estate listings
  • Create 2-3 sustainability-focused blog posts
  • Develop first-time homebuyer guide
  • Add internal linking between related blog posts and project pages

Short-term Actions (1-3 months)

  • Launch monthly content calendar with 4-6 posts per month
  • Create buyer journey content series (first-time buyers, investors, NRIs)
  • Develop project showcase content with detailed stories
  • Add customer testimonials and success stories
  • Create educational content on home loans and legal processes
  • Optimize all images with descriptive alt tags

Long-term Strategy (3-6 months)

  • Develop video content strategy (virtual tours, customer interviews)
  • Create interactive content (calculators, comparison tools)
  • Launch thought leadership content on real estate trends
  • Develop lifestyle content series (interior design, home maintenance)
  • Create quarterly market analysis reports
  • Build content hub for each city presence
